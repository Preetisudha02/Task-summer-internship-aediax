About Bhubaneswar

Bhubaneswar is the capital city of Odisha and one of the oldest cities in India. It is popularly known as the "Temple City of India" because of its beautiful ancient temples.

The city is famous for its rich culture, peaceful environment, educational institutions, modern infrastructure, and tourism.

Bhubaneswar is also a smart city and an important center for technology, education, and business in Eastern India.
