The Capital Pride of Odisha

Bhubaneswar is the capital city of Odisha and is known as the "Temple City of India" because of its magnificent ancient temples and rich cultural heritage.

The city beautifully combines history and modernity with smart city development, educational institutions, clean roads, parks, shopping malls, and peaceful living.

Famous attractions like Lingaraj Temple, Dhauli Peace Pagoda, Nandankanan Zoological Park, and Udayagiri-Khandagiri Caves attract thousands of tourists every year.

Culture

Bhubaneswar is famous for Odissi dance, traditional festivals, classical music, handicrafts, and rich Odia traditions.

Tourism

Major attractions include Lingaraj Temple, Nandankanan Zoo, Dhauli Hills, Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves, and Ekamra Kanan.

Food

Delicious Odia cuisine, street food, sweets like Rasagola and Chhena Poda, and local delicacies make Bhubaneswar special.
