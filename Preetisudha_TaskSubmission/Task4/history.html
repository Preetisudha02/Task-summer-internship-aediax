History of Bhubaneswar

Bhubaneswar has a history of more than 3000 years and was once an important center of religion and architecture.

Ancient rulers of Kalinga built many temples and monuments in the city, making it one of the most historic places in India.

The famous Kalinga War fought by Emperor Ashoka near Dhauli changed Indian history and spread the message of peace and Buddhism.
